Using {{product.name}} Creating A Timer

Once you have added a timer, you can enter a name for your timer, and choose a color.

You can also choose an opposite of the timer, so that when you start your new timer, its opposite will be stopped. For example, you might choose 'busy' as the opposite of 'available', and 'happy' as the opposite of 'sad', so that when you become 'busy' or 'happy', your are no longer 'sad' or 'available'.
